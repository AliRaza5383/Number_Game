Welcome to the Number Game!

  • A number is chosen within a specified range (1 to 100).
  • Players take turns guessing the number.
  • After each guess, feedback is given:
    • "Too high" if the guess is greater than the number.
    • "Too low" if the guess is less than the number.
    • "Correct!" if the guess matches the number.
  • The game continues until the correct number is guessed.
  • The player who guesses the number correctly wins.
